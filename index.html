How to play
  1. 👆Tap any tile to open the action menu — road, building, or empty lot
  2. 🚌Place an action: buses & bike lanes on roads, parking on buildings, parks on empty lots
  3. 📉Each action cuts congestion and boosts happiness — but repeating the same type gives diminishing returns
  4. ⭐Adjacent actions unlock combos — Bus Stop + Park = Transit Hub, Parking + Park = Green Gateway, and more. Watch for the ★ popup!
  5. 🌦️Weather changes each game and affects action strength — rain boosts bus stops, snow makes bike lanes nearly useless. Check the HUD badge.
  6. 🏆Win by getting congestion ≤30 and happiness ≥70 before your budget and turns run out

    🌤️ Weather — changes every game, shown in the top bar

    ☀️ Sunny — all actions at full strength. Ideal for any strategy.
    🌧️ Rainy — bus stops 30% more effective. Bike lanes and parks lose effectiveness.
    ☁️ Overcast — slight happiness penalty on green actions. Roads unaffected.
    ❄️ Snowy — bus stops critical. Bike lanes nearly useless (35% effectiveness).
    ⛈️ Stormy — only road widening and parking at full strength. Avoid green investment.

    💡 Pro Tips

    👆 Tap any tile to open the action fan. Wrong-tile actions are greyed out — only valid placements are clickable.
    🏭 Suppress generators by placing a bus stop, bike lane, or road widening on an adjacent road tile — stops them adding congestion.
    🚌 Space bus stops out — stops placed far apart earn a Network Bonus for extra congestion reduction.
    ↩️ You get one undo per placement (↩ button or Ctrl+Z). Use it when you accidentally break a combo.
    🏗️ Demolish blockers (🚧) for £50 to unlock empty lots — sometimes the best park location is behind a blocker.
